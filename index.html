Our Mission

At Sitka we value Academics as well as the beautiful sport of soccer. We are a private organization and those who wish to attend Sitka must be accepted by the application process. We house students from 9th-12th grade and provide all living spaces and food. Boys and girls are seperated everywhere besides the classroom. From 8 o'clock to 11 o'clock classes are in session, from 11:15 to 2 o'clock there can be anything from weight training, practice, games, tape watching, or team building.
